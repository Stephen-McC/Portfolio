Echoes of Hope

We created this short emotional game as part of the 1GAM challenge. Participates attempt to create a full game within a 1 month period, for the month of February we decided to create something heavily focused on narrative. Echoes of Hope is a narrative experience, telling the story of two friends while once copes with depression and the other must help them through it. We created puzzles that made the player focus on helping the depressed character through their struggle while a voice-over would play giving them context to how their character feels.

Late Panda Game

We created this infinite runner and memory-based game as part of the 1GAM challenge. Participates attempt to create a full game within a 1 month period, for the month of March we wanted to create something with a company mascot. The Late Panda Game is a memory-based game, after the development of Echoes of Hope was completed we wanted to focus on something else that may help the player. This game is meant to help the player improve their memory as each time the game is played the obstacles will be in random order. Players must quickly memorize the obstacles in order to get further and score higher.

The Untitled Late Panda Board Game

This is a board game my partner and I have been creating since we decided to no longer work formally within Late Panda. We have been working on this board game since the end of 2019, it was a mixture of other board games we have played as well as our own mechanics and ideas to change some of the formulas. We have created a prototype which we began testing at gaming cafes around Glasgow and we will be working on an updated version that we will then attempt to have published. This board game was created as a hobby for something me and my partner could easily work on together while busy with other things.

HeadAche

This game was made during the 48-hour GMTK Game Jam, by myself and my partner. It was made in RPG MAKER V and was meant to focus on a narrative as most games created during a game jam usually aim to create a mechanic based on the theme. The theme for this jam was "Only One" so we decided to centre the narrative theme on the main character being the only one who can help himself through his difficulties. We left the majority of the narrative up to interpretation, feel free to play and see what feel.
